ItziPDF Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Developer and contact

ItziPDF is provided by Itzi Group. For privacy or support questions:

  • Email: itzigroup_support@itzigroup.com
  • Website: https://itzigroup.com
  • Instagram: https://www.instagram.com/itzigroup/
  • Facebook: Itzi Group

Short summary

ItziPDF is a local-first PDF toolkit. This release does not require an account, and we do not sell your personal data or document contents.

Files you choose

When you select PDFs, images, text, or camera scans, the app uses that data only to perform the tool you selected. Local tools process files on your device and keep temporary files and outputs in app storage or in the location you choose when saving or sharing. If you open a PDF from another app using "Open with", the chosen file is copied into app cache so it can be reviewed or processed.

Permissions

Camera permission is used only when you start Scan to PDF. File access is handled through Android's file picker or share/open flow, which gives ItziPDF access only to the items you select. ItziPDF does not request contacts, SMS, call log, microphone, location, or All files access permissions.

Cloud/API tools

In this pre-backend release, tools marked as requiring backend or cloud support do not upload your files. If cloud tools are enabled later, such as Office conversion, OCR, AI features, or advanced repair, only files you deliberately choose will be uploaded over a secure connection such as HTTPS to Itzi Group or a disclosed service provider. Files will be retained only as long as needed to complete the job or meet limited legal requirements.

Ads and identifiers

The app may be funded by Google AdMob ads. When ads are enabled, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect or share the advertising ID, device and app information, ad interactions, diagnostics, and approximate information needed for advertising, fraud prevention, security, and measurement. You can reset or delete your advertising ID in Android settings.

Support and diagnostics

If you contact support or use our social links, we process the email address and information you send so we can respond. If crash reporting is added later, it will be used to improve stability and we will not intentionally log document contents.

Sharing with others

We share files or data only when you choose to save/share through another app or service, when a disclosed provider is needed for a future cloud tool, when an ads SDK is enabled, or when required by law. We do not sell personal or sensitive data.

Security

We use appropriate practices such as encrypted transfer for network communication. Local files also depend on your device and app-storage protections. No system is 100% secure, so avoid processing highly sensitive documents on an untrusted device.

Retention and deletion

Local files remain on your device until you delete them, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Copies saved outside ItziPDF can be deleted from your file manager or the app where you saved them. For support-data deletion requests, or future cloud-processed files, contact itzigroup_support@itzigroup.com.

Children

ItziPDF is not directed to children under 13. Use the app only for documents you own or have the legal right to process.

Changes

We may update this policy when features, SDKs, or backend services are added. We will keep the in-app text and public policy aligned with the app's actual behavior.
