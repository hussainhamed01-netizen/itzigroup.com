What Is ItziPDF?

ItziPDF is a local-first PDF toolkit created by Itzi Group. It helps students, professionals, businesses, and everyday users manage documents without relying on complicated desktop software.

No account is required. Supported tools process documents locally on your device, giving you greater privacy and control over your files.

What You Can Do

  • Merge multiple PDFs into one document
  • Split a PDF at custom page points
  • Reorder, extract, remove, and rotate pages
  • Compress PDF files
  • Flatten PDF layers
  • Remove hidden document metadata
  • Review PDFs opened from Android
  • Convert images into PDF
  • Scan documents into PDF using your camera
  • Convert written or pasted text into PDF
  • Add text watermarks
  • Add customizable page numbers
  • Protect PDFs with passwords
  • Unlock PDFs when you know the password
  • Save or share processed documents

Designed for Privacy

Most available ItziPDF tools work locally. Your documents are not uploaded to Itzi Group servers when using local tools.

Files are accessed only after you select them. Temporary files may be stored in the app cache while processing and can be cleared through the app settings or Android system settings.

Made for English and Arabic

ItziPDF includes complete English and Arabic interfaces, with proper right-to-left layout support for Arabic users.

Why We Created ItziPDF

PDF tools are often scattered across different websites and applications. Many require users to upload personal documents to unknown servers.

We created ItziPDF to provide a simpler, more private, and accessible PDF workspace, especially for users who prefer processing documents directly on their phones.

Processing Limits

Limits protect your phone from excessive memory usage and overheating:

  • PDF files: up to 250 MB
  • Documents: up to 500 pages
  • Merge: up to 20 PDF files
  • Split: up to 5 output files
  • Images to PDF: up to 60 images
  • Individual image size: up to 25 MB
  • Text to PDF: up to 250,000 characters

Actual performance depends on your device, available storage, memory, and document complexity.

Future releases may introduce OCR, annotations, signatures, form filling, PDF comparison, Office document conversion, advanced repair, and optional smart document tools. Features requiring external processing will be clearly identified before a document is uploaded.

Important Compatibility Note

Some operations, including PDF splitting on certain Android devices, may export rasterized pages for compatibility. This can affect text selection, searching, hyperlinks, forms, and accessibility information. Always review important output documents before submitting or sharing them.
